Jeff Sutherlin

Co-Founder & President
Jeff would say his biggest accomplishment is beginning UBG in 2009 and continuing to grow it through some of the toughest real estate environments. Laurie McDonnell

Designated Broker
Laurie McDonnell brings over 20+ years of experience in the real estate industry to her role as Designated Broker for United Brokers Group. Laurie has been licensed since 2005 obtaining her brokers license in 2010. Before entering the sales side of real estate she was an appraiser for fifteen years. Her all encompassing experience truly makes her a highly qualified Designated Broker.
Laurie brings an absolute passion to this business everyday. Before her role as Designated Broker, she was consistently in the top 10% of sales at UBG. In 2011 she graduated from the SEVRAR Leadership Program. She has served on several SEVRAR committees such as RAPAC, Grievance, Leadership Development, and Governing Documents. Laurie was voted by her peers to serve as a Director on the 2014-2015 Board of Directors at SEVRAR. Additionally, she was selected to serve as an AAR Director for 2015. Laurie is known for her utmost competence in our industry and her approachable nature.
Above all, Laurie is committed to providing excellent broker support, training and education to her agents and has a genuine desire to help others. She is passionate about the community being actively involved with organizations such as The Care Fund, Susan G. Komen Race for the Cure, Tempe Healing Fields and the USMC Toys for Tots. When she is not working or volunteering, Laurie enjoys spending time with her family, reading, traveling, hiking and taking in the attractions and natural beauty in Arizona.

Tracey Couture

Managing Review Broker
Tracey Couture brings a track record of business success to her role as Managing Review Broker for United Brokers Group. In this capacity, Tracey oversees contracts and paperwork at UBG’s rapidly-expanding Chandler office. She came to UBG in September of 2009. A member of the Southeast Valley Regional Association of Realtors® (SEVRAR). Tracey achieved a career milestone in 2011 when her clients voted her a “Top 10 Realtor®” in Ranking Arizona. Prior to her life in real estate, Tracey held executive positions in the client services industry. Her knowledge about business operations, budgets, and interacting with people has allowed her to transition into real estate sales seamlessly.
Just one year after getting her license, a major Arizona real estate agency named her its Rookie of The Year. In 2008, the same company appointed Tracey to its President’s Circle, which represents the top three-percent of sales nationwide. With a reputation for integrity and compassion, Tracey is proud to call many of her former clients and colleagues friends. She enjoys staying in touch with her professional network. Tracey also is committed to providing straightforward answers to current and former clients as they make important decisions to buy or sell.

Jennifer Aldridge

Admin Hero
Jennifer Aldridge brings a wealth of experience and knowledge to UBG, having started in the real estate field, in some capacity or another, back in 1996. As the years progressed and she became more focused on real estate, Jennifer realized her passion and strength was working behind the scenes, as a transaction coordinator / assistant, making sure everything flowed effortlessly during transactions. Jennifer is the glue that holds UBG together. If you have any questions, start with her first. If she doesn't know the answer, she'll either find it for you or direct you to someone who does!
